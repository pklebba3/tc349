Panic.com

Right off the bat, I was astonished by the quality of the images. The small details that you can see (they really pop out at you) are incredible. The homepage is very clean and simple to navigate. It is very obvious to the viewer what this company and website is all about. I love how when you hover over each of the applications, there is a brief explanation of what it is that pops up. If you want further details, you can click on the image.

I think it is a little odd, in my opinion, that this company has tshirts. I honestly cannot imagine many people buy these shirts. The company provides great services, but personally, the shirts seem a little over the top. Lastly, I love the shopping cart on this site. I have never seen anything like it. You simply grab an item that you want to purchase and drag it to the bottom of the page to add it to your shopping cart.
