My four years at Michigan State University have been amazing. I wouldn't trade this experience for anything. I grew up a Michigan fan, so being at MSU was a little strange. I can say that I have never been a die hard MSU sports fan, like most people who go here.

High School & College

I graduated from Lake Orion High School in 2009. My final gpa there was a 3.75. It is amazing how easy high school is compared to college. My first semester at MSU was difficult. I had no idea what to expect, and I studied like I did in high school, which was a mistake. Look over the notes for about an hour the night before and everything would be good, but not here. I started off with a 2.95 gpa after my first semester, and have raised that number every semester. I should have around a 3.5 once I graduate, which is in May 2013.
