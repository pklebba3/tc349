After receiving my degree from Michigan State University I will be joining Deloitte & Touche. Deloitte is the largest professional services firm in the World. Often referred to as the Big 4, the other main firms are Ernst & Young, PricewaterhouseCoopers (PWC) and KPMG. Comparable to the Big 3 automotive companies, these four companies are the leaders around the world. Below is a picture of Deloitte's training facility in Dallas, Texas. This was built specifically for employees to travel to for various training and educational seminars.

I have always known what I wanted to do for work once I graduated. The summer after high school graduation I heard about cyber-security, and have been drawn to it ever since. At first, I really wanted to work with the government, either for the NSA (National Security Agency) or DHS (Department of Homeland Security), but my internships led me in another direction.

Internships

Blue Cross Blue Shield

My first internship was with BCBSM in the summer of 2011. I worked in IT (Information Technology) in an insurance claims department. Unfortunatley this experience did nothing specifically for my future work, but it was my first true work experience. I learned a lot about business and what it was like to work full-time, 40 hours every week.

Deloitte

This past summer I was a Security & Privacy intern for Deloitte. All of Deloitte's work is client based, so there is a lot of travel involved. Every week, I would fly out on Monday to my client in Pennsylvania and come back home on Thursday's. This was really fun and gave me a feel for what my future holds. Upon completion of this program, I was offered a full-time job, and quickly accepted.

Future

I will be taking the summer off to travel and relax before I start full-time. I plan on starting in August or September, but do not know my official start date. As a Security Consultant, I will be traveling a lot to the client site. This will most likely be out of state. I was very excited to find out about the federal government practice Deloitte has. They have departments specifically allocated to working with the Department of Defense and Department of Homeland Security.
